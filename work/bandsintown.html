BANDSINTOWN APP

FINDING CONCERCERTS

Discover upcoming concerts in new ways: in addition to Local and Recommended, you can now sort events by Popular, Friends, New and Date Range.


IT’S ALL ABOUT COMMUNICATION

New Activity section showcases everything that your Tracked Artists and Friends are doing on Bandsintown. Easily see the events your friends are going to, comment, and share images.


SYNC YOUR FAVORITE ARTISTS

Sync artists with iTunes, Spotify, Google Play, Pandora, Deezer, Last FM, Twitter, Facebook, Sound Cloud etc.



APPLE WATCH SUPPORT

Apple Watch users can also glance at their wrist to see the most popular show happening tonight near the users’ location. This notification will change each day highlighting the hottest local shows.


IPAD COMPATIBLE

Sit back, get some coffe and start getting concert recomendations from the Native iPad App.
